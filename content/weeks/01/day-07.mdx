# Day 7 Lab
1. Think of a doc you remember by its author, not its title.
2. Search with `from:<that person>`.
3. Now find one of your own docs with `from:me`.
4. Note how "who" can be a faster handle than "what".

## Working Example: from: in Action

The `from:` filter finds documents associated with a person across tools;
`from:me` scopes to your own. Verified against `search_filters`.

```text
design review                 → everyone's design-review docs
from:maya design review       → the ones Maya authored or shared
from:me  design review        → your own design-review docs

"Who" is often a sharper handle than "what" — people are entities in the graph.
```
